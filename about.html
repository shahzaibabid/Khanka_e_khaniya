About Khanqah_e_khaniya

Allah Help Those Who Help Themselves

Khanqah_e_Khaniya is dedicated to serving humanity through spiritual guidance, educational programs, and social welfare. With a strong foundation in Islamic teachings, we aim to provide solutions for personal, spiritual, and communal challenges.

Our Vision

To create a spiritually enriched community guided by the principles of Islam, fostering self-reliance and mutual support.

Our Mission

To deliver Quranic education, spiritual healing, and social assistance, ensuring a better future for everyone in need.
