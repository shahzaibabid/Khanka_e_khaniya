Taleem-Quran

Hamari Mission

Hamari mission hai Quran ki taleem ko har ghar tak pahunchana, aur logon ko Nazra aur Hifz ke zariye Quran samajhne aur seekhne ka mauqa dena. Hamara aim hai ke har shakhs Quran ko asaan aur mukammal tareeqe se seekhe aur apne zindagi mein uski hidayat ko amal mein laye.

Ye Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Step 1

Nazra aur Hifz ka course select karein jo aapke liye suitable ho.

Step 2

Hamare experienced teachers ke saath online ya onsite classes join karein.

Step 3

Regular practice aur feedback ke zariye Quran seekhne ka safar mukammal karein.

FAQs

Hamare Taleem-e-Quran program mein Nazra, Hifz, aur Quran samajhne ke liye tafseer aur tajweed ka complete course shamil hai.

Aap hamare online registration form ko fill karke ya hamare contact number par rabta karke enrollment karwa sakte hain.

Ji haan, Taleem-e-Quran ke courses bachon, jawano, aur burhapa ke har shakhs ke liye hain, jo Quran seekhne ke jazba rakhte hain.
